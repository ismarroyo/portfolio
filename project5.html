Centro Dramático Nacional


Series of promotional posters for plays at the Teatro María Guerrero, under the direction of the National Drama Center.


The visual identity revolves around an icon created from two spheres, evoking both vintage opera glasses and the eyes of the spectator. This symbol transforms into an irregular pattern that functions as a mask, generating openings through which illustrations representing the plays are revealed.


Year of creation: 2024.
