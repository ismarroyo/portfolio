Designers Behind Fashion


Editorial project for a book collection dedicated to the most influential designers in the fashion world.


Each volume focuses on a specific creator, exploring both their personal journey and professional evolution within fashion design.


The project includes the complete development of the editorial layout and the illustration of the covers, aiming for a coherent, attractive, and representative aesthetic for each prominent figure.


Year of creation: 2023.
